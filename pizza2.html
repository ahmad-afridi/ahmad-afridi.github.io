🏆 Our Story

ABOUT ZAIQA

Founded in 2015 in the heart of Lahore, Zaiqa started as a small food cart with a big dream — to bring restaurant-quality fast food to every Pakistani. Today, we proudly serve 500,000+ happy customers across 25+ branches nationwide.

🎯

OUR MISSION

To deliver the freshest, tastiest fast food with unmatched speed and service, making every meal a celebration of Pakistani flavor and hospitality.

🌟

OUR VISION

To become South Asia's most beloved fast food brand by 2030, expanding to 100+ branches while maintaining the homemade taste that made us famous.
